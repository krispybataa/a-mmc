Project: Alagang MMC — patient care coordination app for Makati Medical Center.
Undergraduate special project at UP Manila under the CeHRes Roadmap (HCD framework).
Stack: React + Vite frontend, Flask + SQLAlchemy backend, PostgreSQL 16.
All three services Dockerized via compose.yaml in /a-mmc_infra.

## Mission nomenclature
- Backend missions: B<number>-<letter> (e.g. B1-A, B1-B)
- Frontend missions: F<number>-<letter> (e.g. F6-A, F6-B)
- Integration missions: FB-<letter><number> (e.g. FB-A1, FB-B3)
- Patches append -patch to the parent (e.g. B1-A-patch, B1-A-patch-2)
