## Design plans produced

### Patient overlap check
Documented in full — not yet implemented. Key points:

- **Overlap test:** `NOT (A.end ≤ B.start OR B.end ≤ A.start)` — touching boundaries allowed
- **Active statuses checked:** `pending | accepted | reschedule_requested`
- **New file needed:** `app/services/appointment_service.py` with `has_overlap(patient_id, candidate_slot, exclude_appointment_id=None)`
- **Two call sites:**
  1. `POST /api/appointments/` — check before creating the row
  2. Reschedule-confirm branch of `PATCH /api/appointments/<id>` — exclude the appointment being rescheduled from its own conflict check

### Schedule change handling
- `generate_slots()` is idempotent (add-only); if a clinician's schedule changes,
  previously generated future slots become orphaned
- `regenerate_slots_for_schedule_change()` stub added to `timeslot_service.py` with full
  docstring algorithm:
  - **Safe orphans** (status = available, zero active bookings) → auto-deleted
  - **Stuck slots** (have active bookings) → returned to C/S for manual resolution
- Must be called from the schedule-edit PATCH endpoint (not yet implemented)
